Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I am eligible to become a member?

If you feel you have Snohomish in your ancestry you are welcome to email the Enrollment Secretary for additional guidance

How much does it cost?

The Enrollment Fee is $25.00 per person. Annual Assessments (to stay current) are $20.00.

How do I sign up?

Please complete the enrollment application and submit it to the Enrollment Secretary. (then we can have it link to her email)

How can I participate if I do not qualify?

You are welcome to volunteer and assista us with annual meetings, gatherings, canoe journey, cultural events, and more!

What are the benefits?

Each person will benefit differently depending on their invovlment. The tribe is a place to explore your Native American background, meet other indigenous people, learn more about your ancestor's ways, and learn abour culture.
